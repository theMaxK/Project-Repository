What is my Website about?

My website is planned to be about the real estate market, the display of aestetic architectural mansions, apartments and properties and about investment opportunities in this market. On my five content slides I will include sites about different real estate markets, such as germany and the US, architectural epoches of properties and their history as well as historic and well known mansions all over the world.

What is the objective of my Website?

The objective for my website is internally for me to research and dive deeper into the topic of real estate, architecture and history and externally to inform people who are interested in real estate about the industry. I am curious about this Project because I love to look at beautiful and well build properties, mansions and apartments and want to display and transform this passion in a website format in order to share and get connected to other individuals with the same interest and passion.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience is basically everyone who is interested in the real estate industry and it's historical development with it's different architectural styles and epoches. For all of those I hope to be able to create and develop a visually appealing and well structured webpage where they can learn and explore the industry.
